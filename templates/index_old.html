Welcome to Vote2Grow! You can vote whether my plant, Seymore, should be watered today, or just check in on him.

About Seymore

Seymore is a Nerve Plant (Fittonia 'Red Anne') and thrives in moist conditions. It looks best when the soil is kept damp, but not soggy. Seymore enjoys low/medium light, and too much light will make his leaves crispy!

About Voting

In order to vote, you must first sign in with your Google account on the "Daily Vote" page. This ensures that users may only vote once per day and keeps the voting process fair and secure. Your account is solely used for preventing double votes. I do not have access to any passwords and I will never send you emails. If you receive emails from someone pretending to be me, please DM me on Reddit at u/Gabgra11. Any suggestions or bugs may be sent here as well.
