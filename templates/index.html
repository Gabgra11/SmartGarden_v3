Welcome to my Smart Garden

This is a democratic plant care system, where users vote whether my plant, Penny, should be watered today. Penny is a Chinese Money Plant (Pilea peperomioides) and thrives in medium to bright light and well draining soil. It's best when the soil is allowed to dry between waterings.

About Voting

You may vote once per day, resetting at midnight (Central Time). Feel free to check out the live data to make an informed decision about whether Penny needs water today, or vote however you'd like! If the majority votes in favor of watering Penny, the water pump will be activated at midnight.
