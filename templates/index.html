Welcome to my Smart Garden

This is a democratic plant care system, where users vote whether my plant, Seymore, should be watered today. Seymore is a Nerve Plant (Fittonia 'Red Anne') and thrives in moist conditions. It's best when the soil is kept damp, but not soggy. Seymore enjoys low/medium light, and too much light will make his leaves crispy!

About Voting

You may vote once per day, resetting at midnight (Central Time). Feel free to check out the live data to make an informed decision about whether Seymore needs water today, or vote however you'd like! If the majority votes in favor of watering Seymore, the water pump will be activated.

Questions or Comments

If you notice any bugs or have any questions or comments, please send me a message via Reddit!
